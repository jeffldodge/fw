[ About Me ]

I am a mother, a wife, a photographer, and a graphic designer. A couple of years ago I decided that I was bored with my 9-5 desk job and decided I wanted something more out of life. I had already spent my early adult years building a family and now wanted something for myself. I had always wanted to explore and create, like I had when I was younger. I think when life happens it's easy to settle into the every-day and forget what it is you really love to do. For me, that is almost anything creative.

I love to organize and solve problems and make idea’s come to life. I pride myself on a job well done and find great value in constructive criticism as I believe it can only make a person stronger, better and help inform you of perspectives you may not have originally thought of. I believe that collaborative brainstorming is a fundamental starting point of any well thought out project and I like to use it when at all possible.

So here I am now, making it all happen, a senior in college working on my bachelor's degree in visual communication with an emphasis in both photography and graphic design. I hope to be finished in the fall of 2018 and after that….who knows. I am open to wherever the road may take me.
